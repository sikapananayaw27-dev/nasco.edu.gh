Our Story

History of Natilla School Complex

A legacy of excellence rooted in the heart of Abrafo, near the historic Kakum National Park

A Journey of Excellence

Natilla School Complex was established with a vision to provide quality education to the children of Abrafo and surrounding communities. Located just minutes from the world-renowned Kakum National Park, our school stands as a beacon of learning in the Central Region of Ghana.

What began as a small community initiative has grown into a comprehensive educational institution serving students from Creche through Basic 9. Our commitment to academic excellence, moral development, and community engagement has made us one of the most respected schools in the Cape Coast area.

We believe every child deserves access to quality education that nurtures their unique talents and prepares them for a successful future. Our dedicated team of educators works tirelessly to create an environment where students can thrive academically, socially, and emotionally.
